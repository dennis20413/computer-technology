## 💻 Computer Hardware Engineering
### The Physics of Computation
Beyond the code lies the silicon. Understanding hardware is about mastering the physical constraints of performance—from the micro-architecture of CPUs to the parallel processing power of modern GPUs and high-speed NVMe storage.

Key Topics: Performance Benchmarking, Thermal Management, and Hardware-Software Co-design.

Philosophy: Maximizing IPC (Instructions Per Cycle) and system throughput.

## 🗄️ Database Architecture & Design
### The Bedrock of Scalable Applications
Data is the world's most valuable resource, but only if it's structured for speed and reliability. This module covers the spectrum of data persistence, ensuring high availability and ACID compliance across distributed systems.

Stacks: RDBMS (PostgreSQL/MySQL), NoSQL (MongoDB/Redis), and Distributed Ledgers.

Optimization: Indexing strategies, Sharding, and Query execution planning.

## 🔌 Semiconductor & Chip Technology
### Innovation at the Nano-Scale
The frontier of technology is etched in silicon. We dive into the world of VLSI design, the transition to RISC-V open-source ISA, and the rise of specialized AI accelerators (NPUs) that define the next era of computing.

Focus Areas: FinFET Process Nodes, SoC (System on Chip) Design, and Instruction Set Architecture.

Future Tech: Post-Moore’s Law scaling and Quantum Computing foundations.
